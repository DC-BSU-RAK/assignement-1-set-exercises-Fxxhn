A flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with spices, marinated chicken, and basmati rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken (cut it into pieces)
  • 1 large onion ( sliced )
  • 2 tomatoes ( chopped )
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to add taste
  • 3 tsp oil
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves

Method


  • 1- Wash and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  • 2- Heat oil in a pan and fry onions until golden brown.
  • 3- Add chicken pieces and cook until they turn white.
  • 4- Mix in tomatoes, yogurt, and spices. Cook until the oil separates.
  • 5- Boil the soaked rice until it is 70% cooked then drain it.
  • 6- Layer the rice and chicken mixture in a pot.
  • 7- Cover and let it simmer for 20 minutes on low heat.
  • 8- Next Garnish it with fresh coriander and mint leaves.
  • 9- Now Serve and enjoy the taste üòç.
